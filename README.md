# 🚆 UK Train Rides Analysis (Power BI Project)

## 1. Introduction of Project

This project is a Power BI dashboard built using mock train ticket data from the UK National Rail system. The dataset covers journeys from **January to April 2024** and includes details like ticket type, travel time, routes, ticket price, and performance metrics.

The goal of this project is to analyze travel patterns, revenue trends, and service performance in a simple and visual way.

---

## 2. Business Problem

Railway operators need to understand:

* Which routes are most used by passengers
* When passengers travel the most (peak hours)
* How different ticket types and classes contribute to revenue
* How well trains perform in terms of timing
* What causes delays and cancellations

Without clear insights, it becomes difficult to improve operations, pricing, and customer satisfaction.

---

## 3. Define the Goal of the Dashboard

The main goal of this dashboard is to:

* Identify **popular routes** and **busy travel times**
* Analyze **revenue distribution** across ticket types and classes
* Measure **on-time performance**
* Understand **key reasons for delays**
* Help stakeholders make **data-driven decisions**

---

## 4. Walk through the Key Visuals

* **KPI Cards (Top Section)**

  * Total Revenue: $741.92K
  * Total Transactions: 32K
  * On-Time %: 86.82%
  * Delayed %: 7.24%
  * Cancelled %: 5.94%

* **Most Popular Routes (Bar Chart)**
  Shows routes with the highest number of transactions, helping identify high-demand travel paths.

* **Peak Hours (Time-based Bar Chart)**
  Displays the number of transactions across different times of the day to identify busy hours.

* **Revenue by Ticket Class (Donut Chart)**
  Compares revenue contribution from different classes (e.g., Standard vs First Class).

* **Revenue by Ticket Type (Donut Chart)**
  Shows how different ticket types (e.g., Advance, Anytime) contribute to total revenue.

* **Contributing Factors for Delay (Bar Chart)**
  Highlights major reasons for delays such as weather, technical issues, and staff shortages.

---

## 5. Highlight the Insights

* Certain routes like **Manchester Piccadilly → Liverpool Lime Street** have the highest demand.
* Peak travel times are mostly during **morning and evening commute hours**.
* A large portion of revenue comes from **standard ticket class**, but premium classes still contribute significantly.
* **Advance and Anytime tickets** are key drivers of revenue.
* The overall **on-time performance is strong (86%+)**, but delays still impact operations.
* **Weather and technical issues** are the biggest causes of delays.

---

## 6. Show the Business Impact

* Helps railway companies **optimize scheduling** during peak hours
* Supports **better pricing strategies** based on ticket types and demand
* Identifies **high-value routes** for investment and improvement
* Enables teams to **reduce delays** by focusing on major problem areas
* Improves **customer satisfaction** through better service reliability
